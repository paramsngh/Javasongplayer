Program that represents an audio app.
   1.    Downloading content to songs and audiobooks
   2.    Showing downloaded content 
   3.    Displaying information about songs and audiobooks
   4.    Songs to genre, artist and composer
   5.    Audiobooks to author narrator and table of contents
   6.   Playing songs and audiobooks
   7.   Creating playlists and adding the content to them 
   8.   Playing audiobooks and songs from playlists
Note: This project is command based
